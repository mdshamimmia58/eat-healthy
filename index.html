Cookie Rookie Recipies

Becky Hardin founded The Cookie Rookie in 2012 and has been creating trusted, easy, and delicious recipes ever since. The Cookie Rookie is known for simple dinner ideas, delicious crockpot recipes, creative chicken meals, desserts anyone can make, and so much more. We love creating recipes that make putting food on the table faster and tastier for busy families. Becky has been featured in The Kitchn, People Magazine, Home and Family, The Today Show, and more.

Honey Glazed Chicken

This easy Honey Glazed Chicken recipe owes all of its deliciousness to the sweet and savory sauce! Chicken breasts are dredged in flour to add a little crispiness in the skillet, then coated in a glaze made with orange juice, honey, garlic, soy sauce, and ginger, before being baked in the oven. It is a simple dish with tons of flavor!

Creamy Mushroom Pasta

This incredibly Creamy Mushroom Pasta recipe is so easy to make with just a handful of ingredients and 20 minutes on the clock! Fettuccine noodles are coated in a rich and creamy pasta sauce filled with tender mushrooms and plenty of garlic herb flavor. It is the best dinner for busy weeknights!
